Payease

Project Description:

Payease is a smart labor management system built specifically for business owners to streamline workforce tracking and payment processes.

It allows employers to register laborers, monitor loan and salary records, activate or deactivate workers, and track transactions in real-time. With built-in automated calculations, the system gives you accurate financial reports—even when a laborer leaves mid-contract.
